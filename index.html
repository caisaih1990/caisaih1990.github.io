Dr. Saihua Cai

IEEE/CCF Member, Lecturer, School of Computer Science and Communication Engineering, Jiangsu University

Saihua received his M.Eng degree in School of Computer Science and Communication Engineering from Jiangsu University and Ph.D. degree in College of Information and Electrical Engineering from China Agricultural University. Currently, he is a lecture at the School of Computer Science and Communication Engineering, Jiangsu University, China.

He has more than 50 publications in journals and proceedings, including in Information Sciences, The Computer Journal, Information Processing and Management (IPM), Knowledge-based Systems (KBS), Expert Systems with Applications (ESWA), Neural Computing & Applications (NCA), China Communications, International Journal of Software Engineering and Knowledge Engineering (IJSEKE), Tsinghua Science and Technology, ISC, ISSRE, TrustCom. He has served as the reviewer for many journals such as Information Sciences, KBS, ESWA, IJIS, NCA, Computers and Electronics in Agriculture, etc. He is a member of the IEEE and the China Computer Federation (CCF).

His current research interests include: 1) Outlier Detection; 2) Uncertain Data Management; 3) Software Testing; and 4) Vulnerability Detection.

I am looking for highly motivated MPhil students with strong background in Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Data Mining, and Programming. Please feel free to contact me via emails on the page bottom.
